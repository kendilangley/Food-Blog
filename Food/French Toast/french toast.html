Worlds Best French Toast!!

Ingredients

  • One medium sized bowl
  • 3 slices of bread
  • Cinnamon
  • Syrup or Honey
  • Sugar
  • 4 eggs
  • Plate

Directions

  1. Crack the eggs into the medium sized bowl
  2. Stir the eggs
  3. Dip bread into eggs one at a time
  4. Put the soaked bread into the pan
  5. Bake until both sides of the bread are golden
  6. Repeat for the remaining slices
  7. Put cinnamon and sugar on the French Toast
  8. Pour syrup or honey
  9. If remaining eggs, make scrabled eggs
