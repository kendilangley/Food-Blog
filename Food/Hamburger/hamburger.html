World's Best Hamburger!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoon extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil leaf
  • 4 hamburger buns, split

Directions

  1. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat. Mix together the ground beef, garlic, oliveoil , salt, pepper, and basil. Divide into four balls and flatten into patties.
  2. Cook the patties for about 3 to 5 minutes on each side, or to desired doneness.The internal temperature should be at least 160 degrees F (70 degrees C). Remove from grill and place onto hamburger buns. Top with desired toppings and condiments.
