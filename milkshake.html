World's Best Chocolate Oreo Milkshake!

Ingredients

  • 2 handfuls of ice cubes (or crushed ice)
  • 1 1/2 cups of milk
  • 10 Oreos
  • 1 teaspoon of sugar (optional)

Directions

  1. Gather ice cubes, milk into the blender.
  2. Blend for 30-60 seconds.
  3. Add Oreos and sugar into the blender.
  4. Blend for another 30-50 seconds.
